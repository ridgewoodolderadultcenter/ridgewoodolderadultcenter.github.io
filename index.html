Ridgewood Older Adult Center

Ridgewood Older Adult Center & Services (ROAC) is a community organization in Flushing, Queens that welcomes seniors age 60 and older for activities and daily hot breakfast and lunch.

As a member of Queens Interagency Council on Aging, a nonprofit borough-wide coordinating body uniting seniors and services, ROAC is committed to improving the health and quality of life of older adults and their families.
